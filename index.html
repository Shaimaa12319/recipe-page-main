Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beaten egges cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese. vegetables, or meats.

Perparation time

  • Total
  • Perparation
  • Cooking

Ingredients

  • 2-3 larges eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  • beat the eggs
  • Heat the pan
  • Cooke the omelette
  • Add fillings(Optional)
  • Fold and serve
  • Enjoy
